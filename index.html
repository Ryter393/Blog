March Article Experiment

Out of curiosity, I want to write 1 article per Sunday for the month of March. These articles will be "published" by noon each Sunday, but the earlier the better. They will most likely be started on Sunday morning.

Upon "Publishing": Log the date, total amount of time spent on the article, the time started, and the time completed. Check the article off the list of article ideas.

The purpose of this is to see how my personal thoughts and opinions would work as articles, as well as to practice writing and to see how I fare at writing articles in general. They can be shared after March, but should not be mentioned before 4 of them are done.
